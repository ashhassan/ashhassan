Executive Summary

Overcome your performance bottlenecks

You are being asked to support more applications, and new types of applications which are more compute intensive, however, your IT budgets aren’t necessarily keeping pace with the increasing demand and customer expectations. You have to be sure your infrastructure is as cutting edge and next generation as the applications and services you are implementing on it. According to Aberdeen, Best-in-Class organizations are 25% more likely to update their server infrastructure to meet the demands of current workloads, and are able to reduce overall IT costs at 3x the rate of followers as a result (Aberdeen Group)

AMD delivers with better VM Density (32 VMs/socket), better memory bandwidth (8 channels/socket), higher memory capacity (4TB) and more cores per rack (5,376 cores/rack).


Buy just what you need

Many times you are buying two socket servers, as a security blanket for extra capacity. You may be forced to buy two sockets in order to get the IO bandwidth you need. However, most of these servers have only one socket initially populated, and never do wind up having that extra processor populated after all.

AMD Naples delivers a 1 processor solution, with 8, 16, 24 or 32 cores, all with 8 memory channels, up to 2 TB memory capacity and 128 PCIe lanes.


Help protect your data

Many security risks are from internal issues not just external t eats. If you are an administrator and have access to the hypervisor, you have access to all the data for all of the VMs. If one of the VMs is compromised from an external attack, your hypervisor and all your VMs are compromised. If you break into one house, you have keys to the entire street.

AMD Naples is more secure, providing hardware-based security to deliver higher levels of trust and protection for your mission critical server deployments.
